XYZ Galle Resort

XYZ Galle Resort site on a rocky outcrop on the southern coast of Sri Lanka near the old fort town of Tangalle.Golden-hued beach fringed by coconut palms overlooks the roaring Indian Ocean.
Explore the island's rainforests, pilgrimage sites and fishing ports. Dine cliffside, embark on surf adventures, or retreat to the sanctuary of Anantara Spa with award-winning Ayurvedic treatments.
